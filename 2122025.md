### The confused deputy problem and External Id in AWS.
#### While researching cross account access concept in AWS, I kept stumbling upon External Id. 
- Why is External Id used?
  - Short Answer: To prevent confused deputy problem.

- What is confused deputy problem?
  - It is a security vulnerability.
  - A program with lower privileges forces(tricks) a program with higher privileges in misusing its authority. Ref: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Confused_deputy_problem
  - Also refer - https://docs.aws.amazon.com/IAM/latest/UserGuide/confused-deputy.html
